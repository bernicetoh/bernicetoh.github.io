LlaMa Lunch

An interactive AI-cook assistant designed to inspire you with recipe ideas based on your input ingredients.

In addition to delving into the intricacies of the Large Language Model, LlaMa, I took the initiative to conceive and implement an interactive chatbot seamlessly integrated with its successor, LlaMa2. The primary objective was to generate personalized recipes based on user-input ingredients. Rigorous fine-tuning of the LlaMa2 model was undertaken to ensure the realization of our team's specific goals. This undertaking served as a pivotal use-case within the framework of an exploratory project centered around Large Language Models.

FallGuard SOS

A service mobile application that goes hand-in-hand with an external fall detection device. Through real-time updates, guardians can stay informed about their senior's well-being, allowing them to provide immediate help when needed.

Key features:

  • Track fall detection device with real-time GPS tracking system
  • Notify guardian when a fall is detected
  • Notify guardian when the panic button is activated
  • Provide immediate access to relevant contacts
